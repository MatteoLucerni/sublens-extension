Subtitle language ? The language of the Netflix subtitles. "Auto" reads the subtitle track you selected on Netflix; pick a language to force it instead.

Back arrow rewinds subtitle ? When enabled, pressing the Left Arrow key jumps back to the start of the previous subtitle line and pauses at its end, instead of Netflix's default 10-second rewind.

Left Arrow goes to the previous subtitle instead of rewinding 10 seconds.

Auto-pause on hover ? When enabled, hovering the mouse over a subtitle automatically pauses the video, and resumes it when you move the mouse away.

Hovering over a subtitle pauses the video automatically.

Blur subtitles ? When enabled, subtitles are blurred by default so you read the dialogue without seeing the text at a glance. Hover over a subtitle to reveal it.

Subtitles are blurred until you hover over them.

Remove blur when paused ? When enabled, pausing the video automatically reveals all blurred subtitles, so you can read them while paused.

Pausing the video reveals blurred subtitles automatically.

How translation works

  • Click a word for its translation and dictionary definition.
  • Ctrl+Click multiple words to combine them into one selection.
  • Click and drag across words to select a phrase.
